Simple Process

From Idea to Shop Drawing
in 5 Steps

No training required. No software to install. Just open your browser and start building.

01

Describe Your Project

Tell FabDraw what you're building — a gate, railing, frame, enclosure, or custom weldment. Pick from dozens of starting templates or start from a blank canvas.

02

Choose Your Materials

Select from our library of 10 standard steel profiles — square tube, round tube, angle iron, I-beam, and more. Set wall thickness with real-world standard specs built in.

03

Set Your Dimensions

Enter measurements in feet and inches. FabDraw auto-scales your drawing, snaps joints, and maintains proper proportions as you update any dimension in real time.

04

Customize & Annotate

Add weld symbols, joint callouts, notes, and hardware specs directly on your drawing. Rotate to 3D to verify fit and check for clearance issues before a single cut is made.

05

Export & Share

Download a professional PDF with title block, bill of materials, and cut list automatically generated. Share a live link with your customer, inspector, or shop floor crew.

Features

Everything You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

Built for the shop floor, not the engineering desk. Every feature ships on day one.

Drag & Drop Canvas

Place structural members with a click. Rearrange, resize, and reconnect without starting over. Your drawing updates live.

Smart Snap & Align

Members automatically snap to joints, centerlines, and edges. Miter, cope, and butt joints form correctly every time.

3D Rotation View

Orbit around your structure in 3D before a single cut is made. Catch clearance issues and verify fitup from every angle.

Joint Callouts

Add weld symbols, bolt callouts, and connection details directly to joints. Your welder knows exactly what's needed at every location.

Auto-Dimensions

Every member and assembly gets dimensioned automatically. Update one number and the entire drawing recalculates instantly.

Bill of Materials

Auto-generated BOM with quantity, material, size, length, and weight for every member. Ready to hand to your steel supplier.

PDF Export

Export print-ready PDFs with professional title blocks, revision history, and scale indicators. Looks like a drawing your engineer would produce.

100% Browser-Based

Nothing to download or install. Works on any device with a browser — laptop, tablet, or shop-floor computer. Always up to date.

Cloud Save & Sync

Every change is saved automatically. Pick up where you left off on any device. Share a live link with customers or crew instantly.
